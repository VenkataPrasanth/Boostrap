Narendra Modi Stadium

NMS is commonly known as the Motera Stadium, is a cricket stadium situated inside the SSP Enclave in Ahmedabad.

Melbourne Cricket Ground

MCG is known locally as "The G", is an Australian sports stadium located in Yarra Park, Melbourne, Victoria.

Eden Gardens

Eden Gardens is a cricket ground in Kolkata, India. Established in 1864,it is oldest and second-largest cricket stadium.
